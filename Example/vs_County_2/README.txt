Example Pre-Match Pack — vs County 2
========================================
Synthetic data only. No real players.

To generate the HTML pack:
  python generate_pack.py -d 'vs_County 2' -o vs_County 2_pack.html

Players:
  Batters  — Player 1 (RHB, aggressive), Player 2 (LHB, defensive),
             Player 3 (RHB, balanced), Player 4 (RHB, aggressive), Player 5 (LHB, balanced)
  Bowlers  — Player 6 (RFM), Player 7 (LOB)
  3 matches of data simulated per player.
